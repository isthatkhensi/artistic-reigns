Services

Our company offers a range of specialized services to meet your furniture needs. Whether your couch has seen better days and is in need of a fresh look, or you're looking for a one-of-a-kind furniture piece to complement your space, or even if you simply want to restore the cleanliness and freshness of your beloved couch, we've got you covered. Our team of skilled artisans excels in the art of upholstery, breathing new life into your couch by reimagining its design and applying quality fabrics to create a stunning transformation. Additionally, we take pride in crafting bespoke furniture pieces that are tailor-made to your specifications, ensuring a truly unique and personalized addition to your home. Furthermore, we understand the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy living environment, which is why we provide professional couch cleaning services to remove dirt, stains, and allergens, giving your couch a renewed appearance and ensuring a fresh and inviting space. With our comprehensive range of services, we are committed to enhancing the beauty, comfort, and longevity of your furniture, bringing exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail to every project.

Bonnel Spring

One of the most common types of mattresses that one can find. It consistes of numerous coils that form the basic structure of the mattress. The quality of these mattresses is dependent on the number of springs.

Pocket Spring

This is a combination of the spring mattressess and the foam memory mattress. Variuos layers of vicso-elastic foam are laid around the based of the coil-spring-structure thus giving it the quality of two very popular mattresses.

Foam Mattress

Various layers of foam are used to create these mattresses. The top most layer is made of soft foam that can adjust to our body heat. Foam Mattresses are know for contouring the shape of your body.
